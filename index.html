About Zcash Africa

Privacy is a Human Right. We're Making It Accessible.

Zcash Africa is a continental hub uniting four active communities across Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, and East Africa. Our mission is to educate, activate, and grow Zcash adoption across the continent.

Zcash allows users to send funds with complete confidentiality — essential in environments where financial surveillance is a real concern. Unlike most cryptocurrencies, every Zcash transaction can be fully shielded.

01
Education First

We run workshops, publish guides, and host events to make Zcash understandable for everyone — developers to first-time users.

02
Community-Led Growth

Each community is self-organized and locally driven, with support from the broader Zcash ecosystem through the ambassador program.

03
Built to Scale

When new communities emerge in Uganda, Rwanda, or beyond, our architecture is ready to welcome them without redesigning the site.
