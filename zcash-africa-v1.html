About Zcash Africa

Privacy is a Human Right. We're Making It Accessible.

Zcash Africa is a continental hub uniting four active communities across Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, and East Africa. Our mission is to educate, activate, and grow Zcash adoption across the continent.

Zcash is a privacy-first digital currency built on zero-knowledge cryptography. Unlike most cryptocurrencies, Zcash allows users to send funds with complete confidentiality — essential in environments where financial surveillance is a real risk.

01
Education First

We run workshops, publish guides, and host events to make Zcash understandable for everyone — from developers to first-time crypto users.

02
Community-Led Growth

Each community is self-organized and locally driven, with support from the broader Zcash ecosystem through the ambassador program.

03
Built to Scale

Our platform is designed to grow. When new communities emerge in Uganda, Rwanda, Ethiopia, or beyond, we're ready to welcome them.
